Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver?

Yes! I deliver from Juab County to Salt Lake County and anywhere in between.

What is your delivery fee?

From Orem to Spanish Fork, delivery is free. If your location is outside these areas, the delivery fee is based off of the distance from Orem. I do waive the delivery fee if the order total is more than $150.

Can I purchase items individually?

Because I am a small bakery and all items are baked fresh to order, I do require that orders be made in increments of one dozen. This applies to all the varieties of cookies I offer.

Your food looks so yummy but I live out of state. Do you ship your priducts to the customer?

Unfortunately, I do not. There is just no way to ensure freshness and that the cookies would arrive intact. Mama can't have that! I do hope to in the future but as of right now, I only sell local to my area.

How can I contact you for more information or to place an order?
