Mama's Home

Mama’s Sweet Stuff has been in business for over a year now with many of her customers coming back again and again. Lots of time baking in the kitchen has equaled lots of happy customers!

If you want your baked goods the same day, I can do it! If you want wedding guests to come away from your event feeling like they just had an experience with their food, I can do it! If you are looking for business partners to enjoy an array of good food while you’re closing the deal, I can do it! I love to make people happy with food…

Have a look around and see if there is something that makes your mouth water. Then, reach out to me and we can work out all the details to make all your sweet dreams come true.

About Mama

My name is Cathy and I am the Mama that bakes all the yummy goodness. One of my most favorite things about baking treats for you is that I get to know you! I love meeting new people and building good friendships with them. In fact, I already consider you my friend!

I run my business in a cute little town in Utah. For 30 years, I have been honing my baking skills so I can build treats with perfection for you! As a mom of five kids, I know what it takes to create something that will please the masses and it brings me great joy in providing for others. After eating my treats, I want you to feel like you just got a big hug from your own Mama.

Creating homestyle cookies is my jam. I create food for large and small groups alike. Catering and special orders are right up my alley. I look forward to making friends with you through your stomach!

FAQ's

Do you deliver?

Yes! I deliver from Juab County and Salt Lake County and anywhere in between.

What is your delivery fee?

From Orem to Spanish Fork, delivery is free. If your location is outside these areas, the delivery fee is based off distance from Orem. I do waive the delivery fee if the order total is more than $150.

Can I purchased items individually?

Because I am a small bakery and all items are baked fresh to order, I do require that orders be made in increments of one dozen. This applies to all the varieties of cookies I offer.

Your food looks so yummy. Do you ship?

Unfortunately, I do not. There is just no way to ensure freshness and that the cookies would arrive intact. Mama can’t have that! I do hope to in the future but as of right now, I only sell local to my area.

How can I contact you for more information or to place an order?

You can find my contact information in the graphic below. I look forward to hearing from you soon!
